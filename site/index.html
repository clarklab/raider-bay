You're a captain in Raider Bay, Alaska — one boat, one short season, and a bay stacked with the richest fishing grounds in the north. Every square of water is a face-down card. Scan it, cast on it, and haul up whatever bites.

FISH THE DECK

The bay is a deck of cards. Deep water hides the biggest, most valuable fish — but it's a long sail home to the docks before your catch spoils.

BEAT THE WEATHER

Rain fattens the haul. Squalls and hurricanes batter your hull. Read the forecast, roll the die, and decide when it's worth staying out.

SELL HIGH

Market prices drift every night. Haggle for a better cut, bank trophies for ten-fish hauls, and spend it all on a faster, greedier boat.
